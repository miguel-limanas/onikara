# Market Landscape

> Category map. Who plays where. Where this product sits. Refresh quarterly or when a meaningful new entrant shows up.

## Category definition

Onikara sits between a tactical turn-based RPG, an RPG platform/backoffice for configurable content, and a premium dark-fantasy/anime player experience.

## Players

### Direct

- No direct competitor list validated yet.

### Adjacent

- Modern JRPGs and tactical RPGs, including Persona 5 and Final Fantasy XVI as reference points for style, drama, and presentation. `(chat, no artifact)`
- Online RPG systems, guild/mission/ranking systems, and tactical game interfaces. `(chat, no artifact)`

### Platforms / dependencies

- Supabase as platform base for Auth, Postgres, RLS, PostgREST, Realtime, Storage, migrations, and seed. [source/planning/ROADMAP.md](../../source/planning/ROADMAP.md)

### Replacements (non-software)

- Tabletop RPG notes, spreadsheets, manual campaign tracking, and ad-hoc battle sheets.

## Positioning

Onikara should feel like a dark-fantasy tactical operating system for Oni hunters: premium and strategic for admins/masters, expressive and legible for players.
