# Rituals

> Standing meetings, review cadences, recurring forums. The shape of organizational time.

## Recurring meetings

- Weekly progress review — cadence: weekly; attendees: founder/creator for now; purpose: progress, blockers, pending decisions, next steps; notes: `maintenance/` or `.planning/` depending on whether the output is PM Brain review or GSD execution. `(chat, no artifact)`
- Phase planning — cadence: per phase; purpose: clarify scope, risks, acceptance criteria, and implementation order; notes: `.planning/phases/` via GSD. [source/planning/IMPLEMENTATION_FLOW.md](../../source/planning/IMPLEMENTATION_FLOW.md)
- Visual identity review — cadence: during Phase 2 and whenever visual direction shifts; purpose: keep dark fantasy/Oni/premium/tactical identity coherent. `(chat, no artifact)`

## Reviews

- Weekly `/review` for PM Brain maintenance.
- Roadmap review at phase boundaries using `.planning/ROADMAP.md`, `.planning/STATE.md`, and this PM Brain strategy. [source/planning/IMPLEMENTATION_FLOW.md](../../source/planning/IMPLEMENTATION_FLOW.md)
- Sketch review when new first-viewport/admin/player explorations are created.
