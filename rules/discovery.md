# Discovery rules

> How this PM runs continuous discovery. Loaded when the system runs interviews, surveys, or opportunity framing.

## Cadence

- No formal interview/playtest cadence yet.
- Start playtests when a navigable or playable slice exists.
- Review discovery weekly alongside progress review once testers exist.

## Where transcripts live

- Future raw interviews/playtests: `source/interviews/YYYY-MM-DD-<participant-or-segment>.md`.
- Syntheses: `ingestion/interviews/YYYY-MM-DD-<same-slug>.md`.

## Opportunity-solution tree

- Not established yet. Use `.planning/IMPLEMENTATION_FLOW.md` for opportunity framing before major implementation. [source/planning/IMPLEMENTATION_FLOW.md](../source/planning/IMPLEMENTATION_FLOW.md)

## Recruitment rules

- Early testers: players close to the project, possible RPG masters, anime/JRPG fans, and users evaluating clarity of the system. `(chat, no artifact)`
- Keep feedback labeled by participant type; do not treat friend feedback as broad market validation.

## Question quality

- Prefer task-based playtests: "create a character", "find a mission", "understand this combat state", "identify what an admin/master can do".
- Ask what confused the tester, what felt premium/tactical, and what made them want to explore.
